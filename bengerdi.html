In this podcast episode, Dr. Mohamed Bengerdi, a distinguished lawyer and professor in the law department at CU Illizi, shares his journey in the legal field. With insightful reflections and expert advice, this episode is a must-listen for law students, professionals, and anyone interested in the world of justice and advocacy.
