Professor Boubaker Moussa

Education | 40 min

CUillizi by Innovatech club

In this podcast episode, Professor Boubaker Moussa, the chancellor of CU Illizi, shares his remarkable journey in academia and leadership. He reflects on his experiences in higher education, the challenges of managing a growing university, and his vision for the future of CU Illizi. Professor Moussa discusses the importance of education in regional development, the role of research in shaping society, and the initiatives he has implemented to enhance learning opportunities for students. With inspiring insights and valuable advice, this episode is a must-listen for educators, students, and anyone passionate about academic leadership and innovation.
